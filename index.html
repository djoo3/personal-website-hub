A SEQUENCE OF MAJOR MILESTONES IN MY LIFE

This is a compilation of the key moments and experiences in my life that you would perhaps like to know about. Click the arrows on the screen to look through!

SEPT 1998: BIRTH!

Not much to say about this really. I was born in Seoul, South Korea, on September 12, 1998.

DEC 2005: THE BIG MOVE

I spent the first 7 years of my life growing up in Seoul. After attending a local elementary school for 1 year, my parents decided to give me an English name and sent me to live in Vancouver, Canada, for a couple years so I could learn English. As a kid who couldn't speak a single word of English, settling in at my new elementary school was a grueling ordeal...

SEPT 2011 ~ JUNE 2016: SECONDARY SCHOOL

However I ended up really liking Canada after only a few years and decided to stay there for good. I went to Lord Byng Secondary School and enrolled in an arts mini-school program, where I auditioned for the music program. Throughout my secondary school career, I actively took part in the senior school symphony, the student council, the track team, the ultimate frisbee team, and the environment club.

SEPT 2016 ~ PRESENT: UNIVERSITY OF MICHIGAN

Towards the end of high school I developed an interest for computer science after taking a computer science class in Java and decided to pursue this discipline in post-secondary education. I am currently a rising senior at the University of Michigan (go blue!) studying computer science as well as business administration. I have taken computer science courses covering subjects like database management, cyber security, algorithms/data structures, and discrete mathematics.

JAN 2017 ~ APRIL 2019: MAIZE COLLECTIVE

I decided to continue pursuing my passion for music production and joined Maize Collective, a student-led music label. I performed at events and club-organized concerts, as well as help organize these events. Here I met many like-minded musicians who I still work with on music projects.

JUNE 2017 ~ AUG 2017: ELBON THE TABLE

As a freshman year summer job, I took up a service job at a fine dining place in the heart of Gangnam, Seoul. Here I performed tasks including serving, making small side dishes, and bartending. My main takeaways from this job were: valuable communication skills, customer service skills, wine-pairing, and making killer mojitos.

SEPT 2017 ~ 2019: STARTUM ENTREPRENEURSHIP

I applied to join StartUM : a student-run startup incubator at the University of Michigan. Here I met some very driven people who I had the privilege of ideating and growing an entrepreneurial venture with. After spending a year in the organization as a member, I became a mentor. As a mentor, I hosted workshops on web development and UI design for students who needed help on the technical aspects of their startup projects. Additionally, I personally mentored 2 startup groups, setting deadlines, alerting them of potential investment opportunities, and providing any additional help.

JAN 2018 ~ APR 2018: HOVERFLY

Hoverfly is a startup I co-founded that works as a middleman service, linking independent drone photography services with real estate agents. With four other members, we were working to build an app, as well as a website, that would facilitate the whole process. Over the course of 4 months, we worked with agents from RE/MAX, Real Estate One, and Keller Williams Realty. We also came in 3rd at the StartUM startup pitch competition. Unfortunately, due to the school workload, the Hoverfly team disbanded at the end of April 2018.

SEPT 2018 ~ APR 2019: Michigan Advertising and Marketing

Michigan Advertising and Marketing is a non-profit student organization that provides marketing consulting services to local businesses. As a staff developer of a consulting team, I was responsible for providing technology related solutions to increasing ROI for marketing campaigns. I took an active part in communicating with clients, as well as implementing web design optimizations and SEO techniques in order to drive organic web traffic for the clientsâ€™ websites. At the end of each campaign quarter, the teams reported campaign results to the marketing team of Google Ann Arbor.

JUNE 2019 ~ AUG 2019: Mosaique, LLC

Mosaique is a startup developing a brokerage platform that embraces blockchain technology and smart contracts to enable straight-through-processing in multi-party trading ecosystems. I worked in a team of 5 developers in designing and implementing the trades allocation engine of the platform. My work mostly involved developing front-end components and making connections to backend components, as well as running regression and integration tests. By the end of my internship, our team was able to integrate this feature to the rest of the platform in the pre-production environment. As a side project, I also worked in collaboration with a team of UI designers to implement a new landing page for the company.
